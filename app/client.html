About Ave

This is the system messages channel. This is not an online IRC channel, and is used for relaying system messages to you. Messages entered here will not be broadcast to IRC. You may use this to enter commands, such as to identify with NickServ.

As a result, there are no "online users".

Perhaps you would consider, if you're on Freenode, joining #ave-irc? To do so, just open the side menu, enter "#ave-irc" (without quotes) in the "IRC Channel" field, and then click "Join Channel". That's how you join channels!
